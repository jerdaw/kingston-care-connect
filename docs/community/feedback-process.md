# Feedback & Complaint Process

**Effectiveness**: January 2026
**Scope**: Public feedback, content disputes, and accessibility complaints.

## 1. Guiding Principles

- **Transparency**: The process is public and easy to understand.
- **Responsiveness**: Timely acknowledgement and resolution.
- **Accessibility**: Multiple channels for providing feedback.
- **Fairness**: Impartial review of disputes.

## 2. Feedback Channels

### A. Service Listing Inaccuracy

_For reporting errors in phone numbers, addresses, hours, etc._

- **Method**: "Report Issue" button on every service page.
- **Routing**: Sent to KCC Verification Team + Verified Partner (if claimed).
- **SLA**: Reviewed within 5 business days.

### B. Privacy & Security Concerns

_For reporting data breaches, unauthorized information, or safety content._

- **Method**: Email to `privacy@kingstoncareconnect.ca`.
- **Routing**: Immediate escalation to Privacy Officer.
- **SLA**: Acknowledged within 24 hours; Investigation aimed for 72 hours.

### C. Accessibility Barriers

_For reporting issues accessing the site or content._

- **Method**: Email to `accessibility@kingstoncareconnect.ca` or Feedback Form.
- **Routing**: Accessibility Lead.
- **SLA**: 5 business days.

## 3. Dispute Resolution Process

If a service provider disagrees with a moderation decision (e.g., listing removal, edits):

1.  **Initial Appeal**: Contact `moderation@kingstoncareconnect.ca` with evidence.
2.  **Review**: A different moderator than the original decision-maker reviews the case.
3.  **Final Decision**: If unresolved, the issue goes to the KCC Governance Committee for a final binding decision.

## 4. Reporting & Metrics

We track the following metrics to improve our service:

- Total reports received (by category).
- Average time to resolution.
- Number of confirmed data inaccuracies.
- Accessibility barriers identified and fixed.

_(Internal Use Only - No personal data included in aggregate reports)_
